# European Parliament Representatives' Official Contact Information
This repository contains Excel files with contact information of the Members of the European Parliament (MEPs), EU council members, EU foriegn affairs ministers, and Swiss parliament members including official email addresses.

You are welcome to download and access the email addresses to contact these representatives requesting their support for the ongoing Iranian revolution and to urge them to take strong diplomatic actions against the mullah regime.

This repository contains:
- 27 Excel files containing the contact information of MEPs of 27 member countries of the European Union; such as `France.xlsx`.
- An Excel file `eu_council_and_foreign_ministers.xlsx` containing the contact information of the EU council members and foriegn affair minsters of EU countries
- An Excel file `Switzerland.xlsx` containing the contact information of the swiss parliament members.

## Disclosure
This information is published solely to facilitate lawful, individual communication between citizens and official representatives of countries for the purpose of requesting humanitarian assistance.

It must not be used for commercial purposes, bulk messaging, spam, or any form of automated outreach.

If any listed authority requests removal or correction of information, it will be promptly addressed.
